Dimitrakopoulos Panagiotis received his M.Eng. and M.Sc. degrees in Computer Science from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Ioannina, Greece in 2019 and 2020, respectively. His M.Eng and MSc theses focused on Deep learning detection, classification models and Bayesian Variational models, both applied on the Medical Imaging domain. He is currently a Ph.D. candidate student in the same department from 2020. His theses is focused on the combination of Bayesian and deep learning methods for computer vision tasks. His research interests lie on Bayesian methods, Machine/Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Instance Segmantation/Detection, Medical Imaging.
